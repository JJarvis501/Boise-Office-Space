About the building

Shared amenities, private offices, Main Street frontage.

The Sonna is mixed-use by design — private offices and open work areas above, first-floor retail and office space facing Main Street below. Tenants share conference rooms, break rooms, complimentary coffee, restrooms, and the kind of natural light that only comes from oversized windows in old buildings.

  • Break rooms & complimentary coffee
  • Elevator access
  • Large windows · abundant light
