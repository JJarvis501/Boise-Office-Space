About the building

A working address inside Boise's busiest block.

The Northrup sits in the BODO district — short for "below downtown" — Boise's most-walked stretch of restaurants, bars, financial institutions, and shops. Inside, the building offers a balanced mix of private offices and open collaborative areas, common-area restrooms, and elevator access on every floor.
